My first Hackaton

In my 2nd year I participated at a Hackaton in Antwero. We started early in the morning with breakfast and we also got all the information for what we had to do that day.

The goal of the Hackaton was to crack a code that you get by coding plenty of exercises in Java or other programming languages(i chose for java).

At the end of the day they gave away prices for the winning teams, but because it was an event for 3th year students

I sadly didn't get the price but it was a nice day anyways.
